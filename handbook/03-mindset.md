# Mindset — the pledge

*Read this to yourself. Mean it.*

For the next four days, I will be uncomfortable.

That is the deal.

---

There will be moments where the code on my screen looks like Chinese.

There will be moments where I have no idea what is happening.

There will be moments where I want to close the tab.

---

**I will not close the tab.**

---

I will not skip steps because I think I can do it in my head.

Step one. Step two. Step three. Like school maths.

If I try to skip a step, no one can save me — not Claude, not the team, not anyone.

---

When I feel lost — I will show up.

When I feel stupid — I will show up.

When I feel like everyone gets it except me — I will show up.

---

**Showing up is the only rule.**

---

I will write the scope myself. Not the AI.

I will validate with a POC before I let the AI build a single thing.

I am the manager. The AI is the intern.

---

By Saturday, I will think about solving with AI differently.
That is the trade.

*If I keep showing up.*

Turn the page. Pick an idea.
