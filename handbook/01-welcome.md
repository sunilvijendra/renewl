# Before we start, I need to know where you are.

There are four levels people sit at with AI right now. Find yourself on this ladder. Be honest — the whole week is designed around getting every single one of you from wherever you're starting to L4.

## The AI levels

### L1 — The starting line, most people

**You use ChatGPT and Claude Pro extensively.**

You prompt them every day. For writing, for research, for work emails, for life. You've never used an AI to write real code — not even once. If this is you, **you are in the majority.** Don't worry about it. Doesn't matter where you start.

### L2 — You've shipped something

**You've built with a vibe-coding tool. Lovable, Emergent, Bolt.**

You typed a prompt, you watched a working page render in your browser, and you put it on the internet. You know what it feels like when a machine builds you something usable — that moment is the reason you're here.

### L3 — You've built an agent

**You've wired an AI workflow on n8n or Make.**

Something that runs end-to-end without you babysitting it. Scrapes, classifies, decides, sends. Maybe a lead pipeline, a report generator, a Slack bot. You've started thinking about agents as employees — not features you bolt on.

### L4 — Full-stack builder, the finish line

**You've used Claude Code to ship a full product.**

Frontend. Backend. Database. Deployed. You have a repo, it runs, someone can open a URL and use it. This is where every single one of you is going to be by Sunday night.

*And yes, that includes you if you're at L1 today.*

## The promise

### L1 to L4. Four days.

Doesn't matter where you start. The weekender is built so every single one of you walks away from consuming AI tools to shipping an entirely full product by Sunday night.

And the first time you see a green square land on your GitHub profile — that's the flex you'll show your friends. First red-green commit graph of your life. Don't underestimate that moment.

## Warning

### This is going to be extremely hard. And intimidating.

It's built that way. We are going deep into code. As deep as possible. It is going to look like Chinese to you. **And that is okay.** We will start making sense out of that Chinese in the next four days.

The number one thing I need from you — no matter how lost you feel, **you have to keep showing up here.** That's the whole ask. Keep showing up. Keep showing up.

### 20% will ship. 80% won't.

The 80% will drop off mid-week. They'll feel lost, stop showing up, tell themselves they'll catch the next one. **They lose one month of membership. Removed from Slack. Done.**

You decide which side you're on. You don't have to feel ready. You have to keep showing up. That's the only rule.

**Trust me.** If you keep showing up for the next four days — these will be the most transformational four days you've had in AI. Without question.
